Why Africoin?

Built with cutting-edge technology designed specifically for Africa's unique needs

Railway Staking

Our unique Railway Staking system connects validators like train stations across Africa, ensuring fast and reliable transactions across the continent.

PeerCoin PoS/PoW Hybrid

Leveraging proven PeerCoin technology with both Proof-of-Stake and Proof-of-Work, ensuring maximum security and energy efficiency.

Africa-First Network

Designed with Africa's infrastructure in mind - low bandwidth requirements, mobile-first approach, and support for all 54 African nations.

Railway Staking Technology

Just as railways connected Africa's great cities and enabled continental trade, our Railway Staking system connects validators in a network that spans the entire continent. Each staking node acts as a station, processing transactions and securing the network.
